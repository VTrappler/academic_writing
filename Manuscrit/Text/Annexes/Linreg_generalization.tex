\section{Statistical Movement Model: Generalization}
\label{appendix:movement_model_generalization}


In Tables \ref{tab:linreg_mc3}, \ref{tab:linreg_lun}, \ref{tab:linreg_ham} are presented measures of the generalization capacity of the movement model by linear regression. For each individual separately, each pose was by turn left out of the training set and the bones orientations and locations were calculated from the predictor values. These orientation and location values are compared to the experimental ones. The results are discussed in \secref{subsec:model_movement_validation}.
